# MEW Overlay
`<mew-overlay />`

## Overview
The MEW overlay component replaces Vuetifys v-bottom-sheet with acceptable MEW theme options.
The component slides from the bottom and overlays the entire screen. This is controlled by the 
**showOverlay** prop. There are four different sizes: small, medium, large and xlarge. Although
the component takes a title and footer prop, the body content is completely customizable by the default slot.
